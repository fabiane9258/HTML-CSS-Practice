Span and Div tags

They are used for styling purposes.While Span only targets a specific line of code,Div targets the entire block of code.
See below.

This is an example of using the span and div tags.

When styling say changing the background color to black,Div styling will make the entire background color in that block to change, as you can see.

When styling say changing the background color to black,Span styling will make the background color of only that line of code to change, as you can see.
